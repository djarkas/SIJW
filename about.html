About Us

Here at KidsAreTheFuture, we believe in equal and fair opportuities for every child to recieve a fair education and fair chance at life.Our goal is to keep children ages 1-18 grades k-12 on the right track for success not only in school but in the future. Here is how.

Our Mission

Our mission is to actively help children ages 1-18 grades k-12 stay on track for success while bringing more attention to poverty devprived areas in and around charlotte. We strive for excellence in everything we do ensuring that our future will be left in good hands by the next generations finest.

How do we plan on doing it?

We are starting by taking action in two things. One being personal transportation. We here at K.A.T.F we understand not every child has the opportunity to catch the bus or drive to school, and not every parent has the funds to own a car to get to and from work reliably. While public transportation is available it is not always the best option. As a helping hand we will provide every child enrolled in our program with free transportation via shuttle busses. Every child will be at school and have a secured safe ride back home. Two; We know the parents in low income areas might have to work two maybe even three jobs! With that being said we will provide industry leading child care services after scool for children 1-18 grades k-12. Not only is our transportation free and easy to use but now you have a guarenteed way of knowing your child is being monitered and kept out of trouble.
